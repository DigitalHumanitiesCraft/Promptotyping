# Journal

Chronological decision history of the vault, append-only, newest entry last. Content documents carry only current state; the reasoning that led there lives here. An entry records a decision, a rejected alternative with the reason, or a calibration result of a check mechanism.

## Entry format

```markdown
## <date> — <one-line subject>

<What was decided or found, why, and what it replaces. Link the affected
documents. Two to ten sentences.>
```

Moved from a standalone repository into `vault/` at the root of `DigitalHumanitiesCraft/Promptotyping`, next to the site's `knowledge/` base (operator decision). The standalone repository was deleted before ever being pushed.

## 2026-07-23 — Scope extended to full bibliography traceability (operator decision)

The operator extended the vault scope on 2026-07-23: every work the paper cites is to be registered, and every accessible one ingested as a publication source, distilled towards the statements the paper takes from it, and grounded through claims. The prior scope (project figures, genealogy, novelty) treated the bibliography as external references needing no intake; that reading is superseded for cited works while remaining in force for footnote-only resources (tools, videos, standards, repositories), which the source-type vocabulary does not cover and the paper uses as tool evidence. The working register for the sweep is [[knowledge/register-paper-sources]], one row per cited work with access class (A openly readable, B operator-supplied copy, C not accessible), CSL, intake, distillate, and claim status; register, state, topic maps, and this journal stay coordinator-maintained while production subagents write only layer files, per the process rule of 2026-07-19.

## 2026-07-24 — Worked-example finding regrounded after a false empirical statement

The worked example of the paper carried a statement about a transcription model's self-assessment that the current output data of the source project do not support, and no claim in this vault stood behind it, which is the finding: a load-bearing empirical sentence of the paper had never entered the grounding layer. The operator checked the project's output data directly and rejected the statement; the paper now states the documented occasion instead, one transcription of a hastily written Kurrent manuscript in which real words stand for the source word. The error typology of the source project is ingested as a document source for it, and the claim built on it is deliberately narrow, one reading at object level with its dated scope, carrying nothing about model behaviour in general or about the calibration of any self-reported signal. The distillate's open question records that the source's own generalisation about uncertainty signalling is bound to the session it was taken in and needs re-derivation from current output data before reuse.
