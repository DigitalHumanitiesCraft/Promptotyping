# RO-Crate packages research artefacts with their metadata as a machine-readable Schema.org/JSON-LD archive.

## Statement

RO-Crate is an open, lightweight, community-driven approach that packages research artefacts with their metadata in machine-readable form, based on Schema.org annotations in JSON-LD; an RO-Crate is a structured archive of all items that contributed to a research outcome, with their identifiers, provenance, relations, and annotations.

## Support

- [[10_distillates/publications/soiland-2022-ro-crate#^s1]] — lightweight machine-readable packaging of research artefacts with metadata.
- [[10_distillates/publications/soiland-2022-ro-crate#^s2]] — Schema.org annotations in JSON-LD.
- [[10_distillates/publications/soiland-2022-ro-crate#^s3]] — structured archive with identifiers, provenance, relations, annotations.
