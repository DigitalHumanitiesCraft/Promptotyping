# The deep-dive process and the Group class were documented in 2019, ahead of the dissertation.

## Statement

The DEPCHA conference paper of 2019 already documents the iterative, collaborative deep-dive process in which the project team discussed source phenomena with domain experts, and records that the classes bk:Group and bk:Tax were added to the Bookkeeping Ontology through it, prompted by an entry for an unspecified collective (four farmers and a baker who equally pay a tax). This dates the session-driven ontology mechanism to 2019, independently of and earlier than its later account in the 2025 dissertation.
