# No framework of the Spec-Driven-Development wave defines a checking role through subject-matter competence.

## Statement

Where the wave names the person who checks, it names a software role. Spec Kit names the developer reviewing the agent's focused changes; Kiro leaves its approval gate unattributed; BMAD, the most role-rich of the four, carries its analyst, architect and reviewer as agent skills invoked inside the tool rather than as definitions of human competence; OpenSpec defines no such role either. This is the difference that Section 2.5 rests on when it installs the Critical Expert in the Loop, a role defined by domain competence together with knowledge of the model's failure modes.
