# Visualisation in the humanities is a research process in its own right, not merely an instrument serving research.

## Statement

Hinrichs, Forlini and Moynihan introduce the sandcastle to reframe visualisation as a research process in its own right, against the predominant DH terminology of prototypes and tools that casts visualisations as a means to an end in the service of humanities research.

## Support

- [[10_distillates/publications/hinrichs-2019-sandcastles#^s1]] — the sandcastle frames visualisation as a research process in its own right.
- [[10_distillates/publications/hinrichs-2019-sandcastles#^s2]] — prototype/tool terminology frames visualisation as a means to an end.
