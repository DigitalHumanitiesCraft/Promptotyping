# TaDiRAH is the digital humanities' most widely adopted classification scheme, yet it is under-maintained and has been dormant for years.

## Statement

Grallert et al. document that TaDiRAH has become the most widely adopted classification scheme in the digital humanities, while its development depends on voluntary labour and the vocabulary has been dormant for a number of years. Wide adoption and fragile maintenance hold together.
