# The Promptotyping status vocabulary was extended with active and snapshot to name the continuously updated process document and the dated snapshot.

## Statement

The July 2026 audit found the status field not in use as a maturity field across the repositories, with a dozen free values in circulation and two meanings the canon did not name: the continuously updated process document and the dated snapshot. On the strength of that finding the status vocabulary was extended with active (for ongoing process documents) and snapshot (for dated documents), and the free values are normalised onto the extended set rather than enforced against the whole practice.

## Support

- [[10_distillates/documents/knowledge-base-content-audit-2026-07-19#^s2]] — records that status was not in use as a maturity field and the recommendation to register active and snapshot and normalise the free values onto them.
