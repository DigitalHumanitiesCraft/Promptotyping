# TaDiRAH is the digital humanities' taxonomy of research activities, organising DH work under top-level research goals.

## Statement

TaDiRAH, the Taxonomy of Digital Research Activities in the Humanities, classifies digital humanities work by research activity, with eight top-level categories representing broad research goals and finer methods, techniques, and objects below. It classifies activities, not the artefacts or interfaces that carry them.
