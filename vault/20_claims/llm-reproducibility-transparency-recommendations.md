# Reproducible LLM-based research requires transparency of prompts, model versions and settings against model non-determinism.

## Statement

A methodological primer for LLM use in social science makes transparency of prompts, code, data, model versions and settings the precondition for replicable results, and grounds this in the non-deterministic and continuously evolving nature of the models. The paper cites these recommendations in Section 6.3 as convergent with its own relocation of reproducibility onto the documents, the process record and the deterministic pipeline rather than the non-reproducible token sequence.
