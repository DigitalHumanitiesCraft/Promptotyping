# The interface of a digital edition embodies editorial decisions and is bound to its data model.

## Statement

The interface of a digital scholarly edition is not a neutral display layer. It builds meaning and includes editorial decisions and principles, acting as a regulative principle that selects the reading an editor considers most correct. An edition's interface, whether GUI or API, is always closely linked to the underlying data model and the editorial principles expressed in it, which makes interface design itself a form of editorial and interpretive work.
