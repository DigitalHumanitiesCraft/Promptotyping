# A statically published TEI edition of over 16,000 pre-rendered units processes and stores without size trouble on ordinary hardware.

## Statement

The scale reachable on the pre-rendered, self-contained side is documented at over 16,000 edition units, each a single TEI-XML file, with the denormalised serialisation at 25 MB raising no difficulty in processing on a common machine or in storage within GitHub's 100 MB file limit. This grounds the paper's Section 4.3 order-of-magnitude claim about the pre-rendered side of the client-side bound. The 25 MB figure is the person-register serialisation, not the whole edition's source XML, and the report states no measured browser-memory ceiling for interactive data volume; the number bounds build and storage rather than runtime.
