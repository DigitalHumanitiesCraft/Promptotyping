# Semantic matching between vocabularies remains a human-driven interpretive task, and AI becomes meaningful only where grounded in curated, context-rich data.

## Statement

Mapping between vocabularies requires more than algorithmic matching; it demands interpretive judgement and deep domain understanding, and AI becomes meaningful and trustworthy only where it is grounded in curated, context-rich data. This grounds the paper's Section 6.2 boundary: interpretation and contextualisation stay in the expert zone, and the method reaches the same limit from vocabulary work that the documented edition project reaches from entity identification.
