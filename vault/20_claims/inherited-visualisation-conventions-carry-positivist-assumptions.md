# Visualisation tools borrowed from the sciences carry assumptions of observer-independent, certain knowledge.

## Statement

Drucker argues that visualisation methods imported from the natural and social sciences embed assumptions of knowledge as observer-independent and certain rather than observer-dependent and interpretative, so their default conventions are unsuited to humanistic material without critical reworking.
