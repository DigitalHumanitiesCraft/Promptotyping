# Machine-actionable DMPs demand documents that infrastructure can act on, exchange, and embed in workflows.

## Statement

Miksa et al. call for machine-actionable DMPs that exchange information across research tools and systems and embed in existing workflows, and present ten principles to put maDMPs into practice. The document becomes something infrastructure acts on, not a text left unread.
