# Data entry through purpose-built forms merges source work and data modelling into an interpretive act at the moment of recording.

## Statement

A research-database project locates the interpretive labour of data construction at the moment of recording: entering data through purpose-built forms with predefined options is not neutral transcription but a merging of source work and data modelling. This grounds the paper's Section 2.3 point that constructing humanities data is scholarly labour in its own right, carried out where source criticism and modelling coincide.
