# Scholar-Centred Design in the dissertation captured structured requirements from deep-dive sessions with domain-expert historians between 2017 and 2020.

## Statement

The dissertation formulates scholar-centred design as a systematic, iterative methodology that folds scholars' practices, needs, and expertise into the development of scholarly tools. Under this methodology the DEPCHA requirements were elicited between 2017 and 2020 in "deep dive" sessions with domain-expert historians working on concrete financial sources. The elicitation captured the requirements as personas, epics, and user stories, with the personas derived from the deep-dive interviews and the user stories written in a fixed role-goal-reason template.
