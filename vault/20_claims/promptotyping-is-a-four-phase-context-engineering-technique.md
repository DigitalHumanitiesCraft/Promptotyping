# Promptotyping is an iterative four-phase context-engineering technique with form-free documents.

## Statement

The L.I.S.A. article defines Promptotyping as an iterative context-engineering technique in four phases, developing research artifacts such as interfaces, tools, or workflows together with frontier LLMs. The Promptotyping Documents that carry the context adapt to the domain and have no fixed formal prescription. This four-phase framing is a different axis from the three-stage framing of the earlier Critical Vibing blog, which names integrated components rather than process phases.
