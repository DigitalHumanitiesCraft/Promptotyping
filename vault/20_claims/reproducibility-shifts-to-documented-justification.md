# In LLM-assisted work reproducibility shifts from identical repetition to traceable justification.

## Statement

Because LLMs are non-deterministic and a Promptotyping process generates hundreds of thousands of tokens, reproducibility is reframed from identical repetition to a traceable, documented justification, comparable to qualitative research. This reframing meets a prior condition of the humanities, where reproducible research in the strong sense has limited currency because the scholar's subject position is bound to the scholarship.
