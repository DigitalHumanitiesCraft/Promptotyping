# Software engineers who combine research experience with domain knowledge are scarce.

## Statement

Research software engineers who hold both research experience and domain knowledge are in very short supply, for want of institutional homes and career paths, and the fallback of contract programmers typically fails because they lack research experience and underrate the cultural gap between business and academia. This grounds the paper's Section 1 premise that the intermediary answer, even once institutionalised as a profession, does not reach the individual researcher or small project as a matter of course.
