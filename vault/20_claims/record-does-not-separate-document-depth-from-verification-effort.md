# The documented record separates neither document depth from verification effort nor either of them from the attention a project received overall.

## Statement

Document depth and verification effort in the documented projects are the same practitioner's investment, and the corpus audit of 2026-07-23 found them co-varying throughout, with deep document sets present even in the one-day builds and no project pairing deep documents with demonstrably low verification. The observation that artefact quality co-varies with the expertise invested in the documents therefore cannot be attributed to document depth alone; the total attention a project received remains an equally consistent reading of the same record.

- [[10_distillates/documents/revision-audit-a2-2026-07-23#^s2]] — names the project-internal confound and states that the record confirms the non-separability.
