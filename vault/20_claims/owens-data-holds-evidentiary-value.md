# Data is not itself evidence but a multifaceted object that can be mobilised as evidence.

## Statement

Data is not in and of itself a kind of evidence. It is a multifaceted object, readable as constructed artifact, as authored text, and as computer-processable information, that can be mobilised as evidence in support of an argument. The evidentiary status is conferred by the argument, not carried intrinsically by the data.
