# Digital-humanities projects build teams to hold their diverse competences together.

## Statement

Digital-humanities research coordinates academics, students, programmers, and librarians in teams defined by common tasks and shared accountability, with success resting on clear task definition and durable working relationships. This grounds the paper's Section 1 point that team-building is one of the field's documented answers to the translation problem between domain scholars and programmers, an answer bound to project funding and staffing.
