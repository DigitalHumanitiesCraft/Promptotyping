# Sandcastle visualisations are never failures; even when discarded they generate knowledge.

## Statement

Hinrichs et al. hold that, approached critically, visualisation sandcastles are never failures: building them is a dynamic knowledge-generation process, and even a discarded visualisation promotes reflection. This is the disposability-with-epistemic-value the paper reads off Hinrichs for the research process.
