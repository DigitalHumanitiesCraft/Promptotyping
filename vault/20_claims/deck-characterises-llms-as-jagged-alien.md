# The method author's teaching material characterises LLMs as a "Jagged Alien Intelligence", jagged in an uneven capability profile and alien in a non-human error distribution.

## Statement

The workshop deck used by the method author states, as of the 2026-07-19 snapshot, that LLMs are a "Jagged Alien Intelligence". The deck spells this out in two directions. Jagged names the finding that strength and weakness lie unpredictably next to each other, with the term traced to the jagged-frontier coinage of Mollick and co-authors 2023 and the jagged-intelligence coinage of Karpathy 2024. Alien names the finding that the errors fall where human errors would not, attributed to Summerfield, and located on the mechanism level through the Anthropic 2025 attribution-graph work in which the model computes internally by parallel heuristics while reporting the written method. The deck fills the label with named components, unreliable token-level arithmetic, spelling and counting, a statistical, confabulating, biased, black-box method, reasoning as thinking tokens rather than verified inference, and sycophancy as the tendency to confirm rather than contradict. The claim records what the teaching material asserts and dates; it makes no assertion about whether the characterisation holds of LLMs, which would require external sources.
