# The FAIR principles place specific emphasis on machines' ability to find and use data, and extend to the tools and workflows that produced it.

## Statement

Distinct from initiatives focused on the human scholar, FAIR puts specific emphasis on enhancing machines' ability to automatically find and use data, in addition to human reuse; the authors intend the principles to apply not only to data but also to the algorithms, tools, and workflows that led to it. This is the explicit, machine-readable form the paper reads from the context side for LLM generation.
