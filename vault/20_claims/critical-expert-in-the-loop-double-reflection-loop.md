# The Critical Expert in the Loop operates a double reflection loop against LLM failure modes.

## Statement

The Critical Vibing blog introduces the Critical Expert in the Loop as the reflective role that neither Human-in-the-Loop nor Expert-in-the-Loop covers. It requires a double reflection loop, human review together with elicited machine self-critique, precisely because LLMs exhibit failure modes such as sycophancy, their tendency to agree uncritically with the user.
