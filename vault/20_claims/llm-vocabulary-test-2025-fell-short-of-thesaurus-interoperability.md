# A 2025 test of GPT-4 and DeepL Write on specialist vocabulary lacked the terminological precision and hierarchical embedding required for thesaurus-level interoperability.

## Statement

Prompt-based queries to GPT-4 and DeepL Write, tested between April and June 2025 on specialist art and architecture terms, yielded at best general definitions and lacked the terminological precision, conceptual differentiation, and hierarchical embedding that thesaurus-level interoperability requires. This grounds the paper's Section 6.2 footnote evidence that current models do not reach the interpretive precision the task demands.
