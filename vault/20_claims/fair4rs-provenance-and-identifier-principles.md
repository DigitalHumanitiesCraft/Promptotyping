# The FAIR4RS principles require detailed provenance under Reusable (R1.2) and a persistent identifier under Findable (F1).

## Statement

The community standard for research software, FAIR4RS, places detailed provenance under its Reusable principle (R1.2) and a globally unique, persistent identifier under its Findable principle (F1). These two principles are the yardstick the paper applies to the promptotype-as-artifact in Sections 4.1 and 6.3: the provenance requirement is what the method's journal and documents satisfy as a by-product, while the persistent-identifier requirement is the findability condition that unpublished prototypes typically fail.
