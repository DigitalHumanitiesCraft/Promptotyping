# The PRISM prompt configures an LLM's reasoning through a parameterised context document with rated alternatives and recursive descent.

## Statement

The PRISM prompt defines the Parameterised Recursive Insight Synthesis Matrix as a prompting technique that runs Tree of Thought reasoning in a matrix format. It parameterises the reasoning by setting Thinking Type, Focus Area, Depth, and Timeframe before the matrix is built, rates each thought branch on a 1 to 5 scale for relevance, feasibility, and potential impact, and recursively applies its full methodology to sub-problems as nested matrices before a synthesis step integrates the highest-rated insights. This configuration of reasoning behaviour was documented by 2025-01-21, as established in the companion dating claim.
