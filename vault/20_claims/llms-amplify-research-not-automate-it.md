# LLMs amplify computer-based research work rather than automate research, and do so asymmetrically.

## Statement

Current LLMs do not automate the research process; they massively amplify computer-based research work, everything done on a screen with data, code, text, and literature, strengthening the abilities of experts who bring domain knowledge and AI literacy. This amplification is deeply asymmetric: it benefits those with access, competence, and resources and widens existing gaps rather than democratising research.
