# Findings

In the first year after plot metering, the garden's water use fell by roughly a third.[^1] Extending the metering scheme to the planned new plots would likely hold consumption near the 2025 level.[^2]

[^1]: Grounded in [[20_claims/metering-reduces-water-use]].
[^2]: Posit: the reduction is only partly attributable to metering, since the report names the wet summer as a co-factor. Open evidence question: consumption data from a summer with average rainfall.
