# Plot metering coincided with a water use reduction of roughly a third

## Statement

After meters were installed on all plots, the garden's water use fell by 31.4 percent against the previous year. External findings support metering as a plausible driver, while the garden's own report names the wet summer as a co-factor.

## Support

- [[10_distillates/documents/report-garden-water-2026#^s2]] — the report states the absolute drop from 1000 to 686 cubic metres.
- [[10_distillates/data/water-readings-2025#^s1]] — the readings reproduce the drop as 31.4 percent.
- [[10_distillates/publications/example-2024-metering#^s1]] — the review finds metering effects in most surveyed gardens.
