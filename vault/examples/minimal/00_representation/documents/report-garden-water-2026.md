# Annual Water Report of the Example Community Garden 2026

This report is a fictional worked example shipped with the Grounded Vault template.

Water meters were installed on all forty plots in January 2025. ^a1b2

Total irrigation water use fell from 1000 cubic metres in 2024 to 686 cubic metres in 2025. ^c3d4

The board attributes part of the reduction to the unusually wet summer of 2025. ^e5f6
