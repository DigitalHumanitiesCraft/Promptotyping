# Distillate: Knowledge-base content audit (July 2026)

The July 2026 document-level audit of the Promptotyping knowledge bases records what it put into force and which operator decisions followed on the standardisation of function vocabulary, status vocabulary, and document functions.

## Core statements

- The July 2026 content audit put the English function vocabulary into force across the Promptotyping knowledge bases. [[00_representation/documents/knowledge-base-content-audit-2026-07-19#^vocab]] ^s1
- The status field was found not in use as a maturity field across the repositories; the audit recommended registering active for ongoing process documents and snapshot for dated documents and normalising the free values onto them. [[00_representation/documents/knowledge-base-content-audit-2026-07-19#^statusvocab]] ^s2
- The Verification function was found template-ready from empirically extracted skeletons, and no knowledge base yet checked its own contribution claims systematically. [[00_representation/documents/knowledge-base-content-audit-2026-07-19#^verif]] ^s3
- Following the audit the status vocabulary was extended with active and snapshot, and the Verification, Integration and Action-Layer templates were released. [[00_representation/documents/knowledge-base-content-audit-2026-07-19#^decisions]] ^s4

## Terms

- **Function vocabulary**: the controlled English set of document functions (Navigation, Charter, Material, Specification, and the rest) the audit put into force across the knowledge bases. [[00_representation/documents/knowledge-base-content-audit-2026-07-19#^vocab]]

## Open questions

- The source leaves open how the many free status values already in the repositories are to be migrated onto the extended active/snapshot vocabulary in practice.
