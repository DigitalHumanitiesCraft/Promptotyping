# Distillate: Workflows in the SSH Open Marketplace (Barbot et al. 2024)

The paper describes the SSH Open Marketplace and its workflows as step-by-step research scenarios pointing to resources; knowledge/paper.md 4.2 cites it as the field's workflow registry that records workflows as activity chains without a vocabulary for the artefacts that carry them.

## Core statements

- The SSH Open Marketplace is a discovery platform for contextualised social-sciences-and-humanities resources. ^s1
  > "a discovery platform for new and contextualised resources from the Social Sciences and Humanities (SSH)" (barbot-2024)
- It supports five content types including Tools & Services and Workflows. ^s2
  > "supports five content types: Tools & Services, Datasets, Training Materials, Publications, Workflows" (barbot-2024)
- Workflows are step-by-step research scenarios pointing to resources usable at each stage to reproduce the scenario. ^s3
  > "Workflows are defined as step-by-step research scenarios pointing to resources that can be used at each stage for someone to reproduce these scenarios." (barbot-2024)
- A workflow explains a series of actions performed with the help of tools, services, and datasets. ^s4
  > "Workflows explain a series of actions to be performed with the help of tools, services, datasets" (barbot-2024)

## Terms

- **workflow (SSH Open Marketplace)**: a step-by-step research scenario pointing to the tools, services, and datasets usable at each stage. [[10_distillates/publications/barbot-2024-ssh-open-marketplace#^s3]]

## Open questions

- knowledge/paper.md 4.2 states the Marketplace records workflows "as chains of TaDiRAH-annotated activities". The text retrieved at intake describes workflows as chains of steps/actions but did NOT surface any mention of TaDiRAH or activity-classification by TaDiRAH terms. The grounded statements here confine the claim to "chains of steps/actions"; the TaDiRAH-annotation attribution is unverified against this source and is flagged in the intake report as a paper-vs-source mismatch to check.
