# Distillate: Promptotyping with Claude Sonnet 4, the Critical Expert in the Loop (Pollin 2025c)

The blog post of 27 May 2025 introduces the Critical Expert in the Loop with its double reflection loop and documents the two-hour Stefan Zweig Digital experiment as structured Vibe Coding; knowledge/paper.md uses it in Sections 2.4, 2.5, and 4.2. The post glosses Promptotyping as a three-stage framework, which differs from the four-phase description elsewhere.

## Core statements

- The post describes Promptotyping as a three-stage framework integrating prompt engineering, iterative requirements engineering, and critical-epistemic reflection to use frontier LLMs as active development partners. ^s1
  > "Promptotyping beschreibt ein dreistufiges Framework zur methodisch strukturierten, LLM-gestützten Konzeption und Entwicklung digitaler Werkzeuge für die Forschungspraxis. Es integriert Prompt Engineering, iteratives Requirements Engineering und kritisch-epistemische Reflexion, um Frontier-LLM als aktive Entwicklungspartner zu nutzen." (pollin-2025c)
- Neither Human-in-the-Loop nor Expert-in-the-Loop suffices; what is required is critical-epistemic reflection in the form of a Critical Expert in the Loop. ^s2
  > "Weder der Human-in-the-Loop noch der Expert-in-the-Loop-Ansatz sind ausreichend. Erforderlich ist vielmehr eine kritisch-epistemische Reflexion in Form eines (noch näher zu definierenden) Critical-Expert-in-the-Loop." (pollin-2025c)
- The Critical Expert in the Loop requires a double reflection loop, human review and machine self-critique. ^s3
  > "erfordert der Critical-Expert-in-the-Loop eine doppelte Reflexionsschleife: die menschliche Prüfung und die maschinelle Selbstkritik." (pollin-2025c)
- A central problem is sycophancy, the tendency of LLMs to agree uncritically. ^s4
  > "Ein zentrales Problem ist das als Sycophancy oder Yes-Man-Problem bekannte Verhalten von LLMs, also ihre Tendenz zur unkritischen Zustimmung." (pollin-2025c)
- In two hours a working timeline annotation tool for the Stefan Zweig Digital project was produced. ^s5
  > "In zwei Stunden entstand ein funktionsfähiges Timeline-Annotationstool für das Stefan Zweig Digital Projekt." (pollin-2025c)
- The term "Single Page Application" triggers a single HTML file with embedded JavaScript and CSS, whereas "Web App" would steer toward a React-based implementation. ^s6
  > "Während “Single Page Application” typischerweise eine einzelne HTML-Datei mit eingebettetem JavaScript und CSS triggert, würde der Begriff “Web App” eher zu einer React-basierten Implementierung führen." (pollin-2025c)
- A key intervention was to reduce complexity, asking for a simple version without AI support, collaboration, or visualisation that the model had proposed. ^s7
  > "mache eine einfache Version ohne KI-Unterstützung, Kollaboration und ohne Visualisierung" (pollin-2025c)

## Terms

- **Critical Expert in the Loop**: a role requiring both domain expertise and awareness of LLM failure modes, operating a double loop of human review and elicited machine self-critique. [[10_distillates/publications/pollin-2025c-critical-vibing#^s2]]

## Open questions

- The post glosses Promptotyping as a "three-stage framework" (^s1), while the initial description (pollin-2025d), the L.I.S.A. article (pollin-2026b), and the method site describe a four-phase process. The three-stage gloss names the integrated components (prompt engineering, requirements engineering, critical-epistemic reflection), not the four process phases; the two counts describe different axes and should not be conflated.
