# Distillate: Promptotyping blog post (Pollin 2025d)

The DHCraft blog post of 24 April 2025 is the first public description that names the method Promptotyping, and its wording carries the founding account of the method's purpose, its structured documents, its four-phase process, and its stance on the form freedom of the documents.

## Core statements

- The post introduces and names the method Promptotyping, presenting it as a systematic development of the earlier Vibe Coding approach and glossing the name as a blend of prompt and prototype. ^s1
  > "Als systematische Anwendung habe ich eine Methode entwickelt, die ich Promptotyping (Prompt + Prototyp) nennen." (pollin-2025d)
- The post defines Promptotyping as a prompt engineering method in which requirements are developed iteratively through targeted prompting strategies and continuously improved in clearly structured documents. ^s2
  > "Promptotyping ist eine Prompt Engineering Methode, bei der Anforderungen iterativ mit gezielten Prompting-Strategien entwickelt und in klar strukturierten Dokumenten" (pollin-2025d)
- The post sets the method's aim as documenting requirements quickly and precisely for subsequent implementation by an LLM rather than producing finished software directly, which positions the documentation as the primary artifact of the work. ^s3
  > "Ziel ist es nicht, direkt fertige Software zu erstellen, sondern Anforderungen möglichst schnell und präzise zu dokumentieren, um sie anschließend mithilfe eines LLM in Code umzusetzen." (pollin-2025d)
- The post specifies that the documents carry comprehensive context, including the domain background, information on the data used, and technical formalizations. ^s4
  > "Diese Dokumente enthalten umfassenden Kontext, darunter beispielsweise den fachlichen Hintergrund der Domäne, Informationen zu verwendeten Daten sowie technische Formalisierungen." (pollin-2025d)
- The post organizes the Promptotyping Documents, written as Markdown files, into three central categories addressing the questions WHAT, USING WHAT, and HOW. ^s5
  > "Die sogenannten Promptotyping Documents sind in drei zentrale Kategorien gegliedert." (pollin-2025d)
- The post describes the Promptotyping process as running in four iterative, mutually building phases oriented on User-Centred Design. ^s6
  > "Der Promptotyping-Prozess erfolgt konkret in vier iterativen und aufeinander aufbauenden Phasen, die am User-Centred Design orientiert sind." (pollin-2025d)
- The post treats the documents as flexible orientation aids to be adapted to project requirements and declares none of them strictly prescribed, establishing the form freedom of the documents as a design stance. ^s7
  > "Die hier vorgeschlagenen Dokumente dienen primär als Orientierungshilfe und sollen flexibel an Projektanforderungen angepasst werden. Keines der Dokumente ist zwingend vorgeschrieben." (pollin-2025d)

## Terms

- **Promptotyping**: the method the post names, a blend of prompt and prototype, an iterative prompt engineering approach for developing and documenting requirements in structured Markdown documents before LLM implementation. [[10_distillates/publications/pollin-2025d-promptotyping-blog#^s1]]
- **Promptotyping Documents**: the structured Markdown files carrying the project context, grouped into the WHAT, USING WHAT, and HOW categories. [[10_distillates/publications/pollin-2025d-promptotyping-blog#^s5]]

## Open questions

- The post leaves the form of the documents free and defers a detailed treatment of templates to a later post; it does not pose the question of a metadata standard for the documents, so the tension between form freedom and a document standard is present only implicitly.
- The post frames the documentation as the aim over finished software, yet it does not state in so many words that the prototype is a by-product of the documents.
